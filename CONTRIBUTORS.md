### 🛡️ Lead Developer & Organization

- **Sumit Bhardwaj** ([@smtbhd32-ABX](https://github.com/smtbhd32-ABX))
- **Airbotix Technology Pvt Ltd** (http://airbotix.in)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Airbotix is exclusively focused on the Lite3 platform.** 
> While foundational code for other platforms (M20) is preserved as legacy reference, our research and development are dedicated to achieving state-of-the-art results on the Jueying Lite3 quadruped.

**Focus Areas:**
- **Isaac Sim Gym Sync**: Reproducible training pipelines for stable omnidirectional locomotion.
- **RL Stability Research**: PD-control effort alignment and reward shaping for locomotion.
- **Multi-Modal SLAM Strategy**: Training policies for Fast-LIO state estimation sync.
